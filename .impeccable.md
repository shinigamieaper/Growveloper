# Growveloper — Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Founders (early-stage to seed) and SMB owners (10–100 people) who need a trusted technical growth partner. They're results-oriented and busy — they scan, not read. They're savvy enough to recognize craft, and they'll make a gut decision in seconds about whether Growveloper can handle their level. They arrive with a problem (no growth system, bad conversion, need dev + marketing integrated) and need to leave feeling: *"these people get it, and they're clearly good."*

### Brand Personality
**Ambitious. Bold. Customer-Centered.**

Growveloper speaks the client's language — every headline is about what *they* get, not what Growveloper does. The voice is direct and confident without being arrogant. It makes strong statements. It doesn't hedge. It sounds like a senior founder who's solved this problem before and is being real with you.

Anti-references (hard avoid):
- Generic agency: no stock photos, no pastel gradient blobs, no hero illustrations, no "We help brands grow" copy
- Corporate/enterprise: no navy + white, no clip-art icons, no stiff grid layouts, no procurement language
- Raw hacker/dev: not just terminal green on black, not pure brutalist, not dev-portfolio minimalism

### Aesthetic Direction
**"Aqua Authority"** — dark-first premium with a technical edge and confident restraint.

- **Base**: Near-black (`#0a0a0a`) backgrounds with layered dark surfaces (`#111`, `#1a1a1a`)
- **Accent**: Teal spectrum (`#aeeeee` light → `#5ab1b1` mid → `#2b7575` dark) — signals growth, momentum, precision
- **Surface treatment**: Glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur`, semi-transparent borders) — creates depth without heaviness
- **Typography**: GeneralSans (headings — geometric, confident) + Gambetta (body — warm serif, human) + JetBrains Mono (code/data)
- **Motion**: GSAP ScrollTrigger reveals, Lottie illustrations, purposeful not decorative — every animation earns its place
- **Gradient text**: Teal gradient on key headline words for visual punch without color overload
- **Light mode**: Exists but dark is the designed-for experience

### Design Principles

1. **Competence is visible** — the quality of spacing, animation, and typography IS the portfolio. Every pixel signals whether Growveloper can be trusted with someone's business.

2. **One bold move per screen** — each section makes one clear, strong statement. No competing headlines, no paragraph-before-the-headline, no clutter. White space is confidence.

3. **Client-first language everywhere** — headlines frame the visitor's outcome, not Growveloper's service. "Your pipeline, systematized" over "We build growth systems."

4. **Dark with intention** — the dark palette is a deliberate premium signal, not a developer aesthetic. Every light element (teal accent, glass card, gradient text) should feel earned and precise.

5. **Bold restraint** — strong typographic scale, decisive layout moves, but always room to breathe. Not maximalist chaos. Not nervous minimalism. Assured.

## Token Reference

```css
/* Brand */
--brand-light: #aeeeee;
--brand-mid: #5ab1b1;
--brand-dark: #2b7575;

/* Surfaces (dark mode — primary) */
--bg-primary: #0a0a0a;
--bg-secondary: #111111;
--bg-tertiary: #1a1a1a;

/* Text */
--text-primary: #f8f8f8;
--text-secondary: #a0a0a0;
--text-tertiary: #8a8a8a;

/* Glass */
--glass-bg: rgba(255,255,255,0.05);
--glass-border: rgba(255,255,255,0.1);
```

## Fonts

- `--font-heading`: GeneralSans (300–700) — all headings
- `--font-body`: Gambetta (300–700, serif) — body copy
- `--font-mono`: JetBrains Mono — code, data, terminal contexts
- Sizing: fluid `clamp(min, vw, max)` — no breakpoint overrides

## Motion Rules

- GSAP ScrollTrigger for scroll-driven reveals
- Lottie for complex illustrations (scroll-triggered on mobile, auto on desktop)
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected — all durations collapse to 0.01ms
- Easing: ease-out for entrances, ease-in for exits, no linear
